Case Study 01

From 1 Brand to 4: How a Rochester Auto Group Scaled with BCG

The Challenge

Troy Bhur started with a single used car dealership and needed a marketing partner to help him grow. He had no digital presence, no systematic marketing approach, and was competing against established dealerships with bigger budgets.

What BCG Did

Developed and executed a comprehensive digital marketing strategy including professional website design and management for all 4 brands, video production and social media marketing across multiple platforms, paid advertising management (Google Ads, Meta Ads), online reputation management (Google Reviews, Yelp), and brand identity development for each new business as Troy expanded.

The Results

Expanded from 1 business to 4 distinct brands
7+ year client relationship — BCG's longest engagement
Consistent lead generation and online visibility across all businesses
“

"BCG has been a game changer. We started with one brand and now we've expanded to four businesses. We've seen amazing results in our marketing and sales and I highly recommend them."

— Troy Bhur, Owner, Southpoint Motors

Case Study 02

From Food Truck to Restaurant Empire: A Rochester Growth Story

The Challenge

The founder contacted BCG in 2020 with a vision: start a mobile food business. No existing business infrastructure, no marketing, no operational framework. Just a great concept and a lot of ambition.

What BCG Did

This engagement went far beyond marketing. BCG served as a fractional COO and strategic advisor, providing business strategy and launch planning, brand identity development, digital marketing (social media, website, paid ads), project management for the brick-and-mortar restaurant buildout, and ongoing operational consulting and business process design.

The Results

Grew from concept to 2 mobile food units + brick-and-mortar restaurant
Built a recognizable Rochester brand from scratch
End-to-end advisory: strategy, marketing, PM, and operations
“

"I first contacted BCG in 2020, when I needed help to start a new food truck business. Since then, Taco Lab now has two full operating mobile food units and a brick-and-mortar sister restaurant. They are critical to our continued success."

— Owner, Taco Lab
